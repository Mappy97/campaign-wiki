# Contact Reyes

**Status:** Active  
**Type:** Fetch/Communication Quest

## Description

The party needs to send a message to Reyes to have him reconvene secretly and return to the city to meet with the party.

## Objectives

- [ ] Send a bird to Reyes via histense swab
- [ ] Wait for Reyes to respond
- [ ] Meet with Reyes secretly

## Notes

Mentioned during the train/boulder incident on January 24, 2026. The party decided not to send a search party to find him in the woods as they didn't have anything to follow and would be easy targets.
