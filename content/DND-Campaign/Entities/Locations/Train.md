# The Train

**Type:** Transportation Vehicle / Steam Tank

## Description

The party has encountered and utilized trains multiple times during the campaign. The train serves as both transportation and has been the site of combat encounters.

## Notable Events

- **Train/Boulder Encounter (Jan 24, 2026):** Party was on a train that collided into woods. Combat with bandits and the Iron Drake occurred on top of the moving train.
- Train was destroyed/left behind during escape from enemies

## Known Connections

- Connected to the **Woods** location
- Connected to **Prison** (possibly as transportation route)
