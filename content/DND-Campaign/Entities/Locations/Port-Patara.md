# Port Patara

**Type:** Port / Settlement  
**Status:** Active

## Description

A port town that the party has visited. It's located near water with several little rivers. There's a little bridge to the east of the port.

## Notable Features

- **Little Rivers:** Several small rivers flowing through the area
- **Bridge:** A small bridge to the east
- **Escort Route:** The road to the city passes through here, with citizens sometimes being escorted by armed guards

## Notable Events

- **Citizens Escort:** The party saw guards escorting shaken citizens into the city who had a "run-in with some beastmen up the road"
- The party has traveled through this area

## Connected Locations

- **The City** - Connected via road
- **Whistlebrush** - Area where beastmen attack

## Notes
