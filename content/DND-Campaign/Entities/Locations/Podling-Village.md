# Podling Village

**Type:** Hidden Village  
**Status:** Active

## Description

The home village of the podlings, hidden deep in a forest. The entrance is a tunnel formed by tree roots under a large tree — only visible when Frog Spittle leads you there. The village has its own rules, a guardian, a potion shop, and is protected by the deity Gleed.

## Access

- **Hidden Entrance:** A hole in the roots of a tree forming a tunnel, only accessible with Frog Spittle as guide
- **Guardian:** A guardian controls entry and requires Frog Spittle to vouch for visitors ("you know the rules of the land")

## Notable Features

- **Deity Gleed:** The podling deity whose presence was felt during a party visit — light cascaded down, touching the podlings and the party
- **Potion Shop:** Has a potion seller with various options and leftover potions available
- **Podling Children:** Multiple children live here
- **Village Rule:** "Do not cause any trouble" is strictly enforced

## Notable Events

- **First Visit:** Party escorted Frog Spittle home, accompanied by Erasmus. They met the guardian.
- **Gleed Manifestation:** The deity Gleed appeared, healing Jimothy's scars and blessing the party.
- **Gordon Transformed:** "Frog Spittle said your village would be able to help our friend Gordon here transform back into a dwarf" — Gordon apparently had been transformed.
- **Dice Game:** Gordon played a dice game with the podlings and won 35 gold with his "infinite water thing."

## Relationships

- **Frog Spittle** - Village member and party guide
- **The Party** - Visitors; have some standing with the village

## Notes

- The podlings are small creatures with children
- They trust Frog Spittle
- Gordon is apparently treated like a pirate legend by the podling children
