# The City

**Type:** Settlement / City  
**Status:** Active

## Description

The main city where the campaign takes place. The party has traveled through the city multiple times and has ongoing relations with various city figures including the Mayor.

## Notable Features

- **The Mayor's Manor** - The mayor has been staying in his manor and recently received acolytes
- **City Streets** - The party has traveled through multiple times
- **Front Gate** - Exit point from the city
- **Random Magic Users** - People in the city have been manifesting magical abilities (shooting flames, etc.)

## Notable Events

- **Magic Awakening:** Random people in the city have started manifesting magical abilities - shooting sparks of flames and simple spells. This seems to be a curiosity among the townsfolk.

## Connected Locations

- **Bandit Camp** - Outside the city
- **Whistlebrush** - Location of brigands

## Quests & Relations

- The party has dealings with the Mayor
- The party works with bandits (morally gray)
