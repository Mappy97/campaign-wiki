# Reyes (Arrayes / Araeus)

**Also known as:** Arrayes, Araeus  
**Status:** Active / Missing (post-train incident)

## Description

Reyes (also called Arrayes or Araeus) is a party ally who accompanies them on important missions. He is skilled enough that the party trusts him on sensitive operations. After the train/boulder incident (Jan 24, 2026) he disappeared into the woods and the party lost track of him.

## Notable Events

- **Podling Village Expedition:** Traveled with the party and Frog Spittle to the Podling Village. Elysium confirmed "have Erasmus and Araeus right?" and DM confirmed he was present.
- **Old Rusty Sword:** Reyes pulled out "an old, rusty short sword" from his bag during a tense moment.
- **Train/Boulder Incident (Jan 24, 2026):** Was in the woods somewhere near the elf mage's location. After the elf mage fled, the party speculated Reyes was in the woods. They debated sending a search party.
- **Post-Boulder Plan:** The party planned to send a bird to Reyes via "histense swab" telling him to "reconvene secretly back to the city."

## Relationships

- **The Party** - Trusted ally
- **Frog Spittle** - Traveled together to Podling Village
- **Erasmus** - Fellow party ally
