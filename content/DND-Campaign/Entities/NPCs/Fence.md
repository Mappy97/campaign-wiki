# The Fence

**Type:** Black Market Contact  
**Status:** Active

## Description

A fence (black market dealer) that works with the bandits and the party to sell stolen goods.

## Notable Events

- **Visiting Bandit Camp:** The fence visits the bandit camp in person to conduct transactions
- **Gold Payments:** The fence pays the bandits for goods, which then goes to the party's coffers
- **Variable Methods:** Each fence operates differently - this one meets in person

## Business Details

- Handles stolen goods from bandit raids
- The party tracks gold through "coffers" system
- The fence brings money to pay for goods

## Notes

- Important economic NPC for the party
- Connected to bandit operations
