# Witch Hunter

**Type:** Government Operative / Authority Figure  
**Status:** Active

## Description

The Witch Hunter is a government operative who investigates and hunts magic users and enemies of the state. The party went "face to face" with the Witch Hunter in what appears to have been a tense encounter.

## Notable Events

- **Direct Encounter:** The party encountered the Witch Hunter directly: "Went face to face with the witch hunter"
- **Deluge Investigation:** When questioning the party's loyalty, someone suggested "we should call upon the witch hunter — how do we know that you're not a member of the Deluge?"
- **Prison Battle:** During a prison-related mission involving stairs, the Witch Hunter fired a large glimmering crossbow bolt at a party member
