# Erasmus

**Type:** Bandit / Party Ally  
**Status:** Active

## Description

Erasmus is a member of the party's bandit crew and a trusted ally. He was involved in the early scouting mission into the forest and later traveled with the party to the podling village. The DM noted "it was me who found this little podling in the first place" - Erasmus found Frog Spittle originally.

## Notable Events

- **Forest Scouting:** Erasmus led the party's bandits into the forest to retrieve Frog Spittle, but they were shot with arrows (carefully aimed to only maim). No fatalities. They had to retreat.
- **Traveled with Party:** Went with the party and Frog Spittle to the podling village.
- **Magical Pulse:** In a later session, "the magical pulse seems to be coming from Erasmus" — he may have developed or had latent magical abilities.
- **Financial Questions:** Elysium asked: "Have you been in need of money, since we've running the show Erasmus?" — suggesting he's been under the party's leadership.

## Relationships

- **The Party** - Works under them
- **Frog Spittle** - Found her originally
- **Bandit Crew** - Part of the force (21 bandits, 16 upgraded)

## Notes

- Loyal to the party
- Was part of the failed forest scouting mission
