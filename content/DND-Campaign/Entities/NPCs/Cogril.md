# Cogril

**Type:** Ungor / Beastman Translator  
**Status:** Active (unknown allegiance)

## Description

Cogril is an ungor (beastman) who serves as a translator and scribe for the ungor leader. Unlike the other physically powerful ungors, Cogril is described as frail, armed with scrolls and writing utensils rather than weapons. He interprets between the beastman leader and the party.

## Appearance

- Small frame compared to other ungors
- Carries scrolls and writing implements in a sling
- Frail build — described as "less jacked" than his appearance suggests

## Notable Events

- **Party Capture (Aug 17):** The party was captured by ungors. Cogril appeared as translator for the ungor leader. He translated the leader's demands and negotiated.
- **Negotiation:** After Jimothy made a powerful cry that got the leader's attention, Cogril negotiated — the party could go free if they handed over a special weapon (axe).
- **Threat Translation:** Relayed the leader's message: "hand over the weapon you carry" or face execution

## Notes

- Uses polite, formal language
- Seems somewhat sympathetic to the party ("before I translate that for you, I will say that appeals to the moral sensibilities of my leader have not boded well...")
