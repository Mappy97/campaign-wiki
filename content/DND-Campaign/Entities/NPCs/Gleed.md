# Gleed

**Type:** Deity  
**Status:** Active

## Description

Gleed is the deity worshipped by the podlings and their village. The party witnessed Gleed's presence during a visit to the Podling Village, where light cascaded down upon the podlings and the party.

## Manifestation

- **Light Form:** Gleed's presence was felt as light slowly cascading down, with raindrops of light landing on podlings and the party
- **Healing:** Gleed healed Jimothy's scars during this manifestation
- **Overwatch:** Gleed watches over the "agrin" (a magical presence/creature connected to the party)

## Notable Events

- **Podling Village Manifestation:** "The deity is present... it is the presence of Gleed." Light fell on the party, healing Jimothy's scars.
- **Guardian Invocation:** The village guardian invoked "by the will of Gleed" when warning Frog Spittle not to cause trouble.
- **Interaction with Other Deities:** Elysium asked about Gleed's representative noticing "interactions of its power now interacting with other deities through us" — suggesting the party carries marks of multiple deities.

## Notes

- Benevolent deity
- Connected to podlings and their forest
- The party has received blessings from Gleed
