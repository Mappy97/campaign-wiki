# Elf Mage

**Type:** Magic User / Enemy  
**Race:** Elf

## Description

An elf witch/mage encountered during the train/boulder incident. She was casting spells at the party but was interrupted and fled.

## Notable Events

- **Train/Boulder Incident (Jan 24, 2026):** The elf mage was standing and casting spells at the party
- She suddenly stopped casting, looked terrified, turned back to the woods, and ran
- She "burst into smoke and disappeared" - suggesting some kind of teleportation or transformation magic

## Notes

- Fled during the chaos of the train crash
- Party speculated she might have been connected to Reyes or the creature they saw earlier
