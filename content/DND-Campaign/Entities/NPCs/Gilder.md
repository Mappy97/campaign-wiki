# N. Gilder (General Gilder)

**Type:** Whistlebrush Brigand Leader  
**Status:** Active (Enemy)

## Description

N. Gilder is the leader of the Whistlebrush brigands — rival outlaws the party has encountered on the road near Port Patara. The party has had at least one prior conversation with Gilder.

## Notable Events

- **Road Encounter:** The party encountered Whistlebrush brigands on the road leaving Port Patara. The brigands were doing patrols "sponsored by the mayor" asking questions about travelers.
- **Thieves' Cant Exchange:** During this encounter, Gordon and Elysium used thieves' cant. One brigand whispered "frog met" in thieves' cant. Gordon and Elysium responded in thieves' cant and the brigands dropped their act.
- **The Ring:** Gilder asked about a "shiny, fancy ring" that Gordon was wearing.

## Relationships

- **Whistlebrush Brigands** - Leader
- **The Mayor** - Claims to work for him
- **The Party** - Complicated; known acquaintances
