# Frog Spittle

**Type:** Podling (Creature) / Party Companion  
**Status:** Active

## Description

Frog Spittle is a podling companion of the party. She was being held at the bandit camp when the campaign began, and the party traveled to retrieve her. She serves as a guide and has connections to the podling village and to the deity Gleed.

## Background

The DM mentioned: "it was me who found this little podling in the first place." She knows the rules of the podling village and acts as the party's guide into podling territory.

## Notable Events

- **Held at Bandit Camp (Session 1):** Frog Spittle was being held at the bandit camp. The party's first major goal was to retrieve her.
- **Erasmus Scouting Fail:** Erasmus (party's bandit ally) tried to enter the forest to find her but was shot with arrows (carefully aimed to maim only) and had to retreat.
- **Retrieved:** The party successfully retrieved Frog Spittle from the bandit camp area.
- **Podling Village Guide:** Led the party to the podling village through a hidden tunnel in tree roots.
- **Voice of a Woman:** A female voice was heard giving Frog Spittle instructions about "keeping an eye" and "a signal."
- **Potions:** Has connections to a potion shop within the podling village.
- **Gleed Connection:** Present when the deity Gleed's presence was felt.

## Relationships

- **The Party** - Close ally and guide
- **Erasmus** - Knows him; he found her originally  
- **Podling Village** - Her home community
- **Gleed** - The deity of the podling village
