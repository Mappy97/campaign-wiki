# Mayor Poppy

**Type:** Mayor / Authority Figure  
**Race:** Podling  
**Status:** Active

## Description

Mayor Poppy is the mayor of the Podling Village. She was elected because she is the tallest podling in the group. She presides over village events including the festival the party attended.

## Personality

- Tolerant but doesn't want trouble from visitors ("take it easy, don't do anything too crazy")
- Presided over the village festival
- Stepped aside when the creature came to address the podium

## Notable Events

- **Festival Hosting:** Welcomed the party to the village festival: "since you're here, you might as well join in"
- **Village Leadership:** Stepped down from the podium when a creature came to address the crowd; sat with Sergeant Speckle

## Notes

- Elected by height (tallest podling)
- Sits with Sergeant Speckle at events
