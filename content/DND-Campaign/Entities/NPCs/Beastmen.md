# Beastmen

**Type:** Monster / Faction  
**Status:** Hostile

## Description

Beastmen are hostile creatures that have attacked citizens near Port Patara. They roam the roads and pose a threat to travelers.

## Notable Events

- **Attack on Citizens:** Beastmen attacked a family and citizens on the road near Port Patara
- The attacked citizens were escorted to the city by armed guards
- The party heard about this encounter

## Known Locations

- **Roads** - Their hunting grounds
