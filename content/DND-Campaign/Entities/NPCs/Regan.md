# Regan

**Type:** NPC / Social Operative  
**Status:** Active

## Description

Regan is a charismatic, socially adept NPC known to Jimothy. He is excellent at navigating social situations — schmoozing at parties, playing guitar, and steering conversations. He attends high-profile parties and events.

## Notable Events

- **Party Schmooze:** At a major party, Regan guided a high-profile couple through the event with a "disarming smile and tactful words," keeping admirers away
- **Guitar Player:** Plays guitar at social events
- **Jimothy Connection:** Jimothy personally knows Regan and vouched for him: "except for Regan, and he's great, so let's go"
- **Fumble Catcher:** During a social event, "Regan tries to catch the fumble" when the mayor made a mistake

## Relationships

- **Jimothy** — Known acquaintance/friend
- **The Mayor** — Works with or near the mayor at events
