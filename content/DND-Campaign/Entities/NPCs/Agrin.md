# Agrin

**Type:** Deity / Divine Presence  
**Status:** Active

## Description

Agrin is a deity whose presence is particularly associated with Elysium. The feeling of Agrin lingers like a warm, persistent sensation. Elysium has had multiple recent interactions with Agrin.

## Nature

- Described as a "lingering feeling" — like going to church and having that feeling stick with you even after you leave
- Connected to Elysium specifically
- Part of a broader world of deities including Gleed

## Notable Events

- **Gleed Interaction:** When Gleed manifested in the Podling Village, Elysium asked whether Gleed's representative noticed "interactions of its power now interacting with other deities through us... watching over the agrin present"
- **Divine Energy:** Elysium carries Agrin's divine energy which interacts with Gleed's energy

## Relationships

- **Elysium** — Primary vessel/devotee
- **Gleed** — Other deity the party has encountered

## Notes

- Elysium's personal deity
- "Agrin to divines, to whoever the f*** this is" — Elysium invokes Agrin when calling on divine power
