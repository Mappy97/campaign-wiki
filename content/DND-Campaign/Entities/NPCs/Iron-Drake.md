# Iron Drake

**Type:** Dragon / Creature  
**Alignment:** Hostile

## Description

An Iron Drake encountered during the train/boulder incident. The drake was equipped with a flamethrower and attacked the party while they were on the moving train.

## Abilities

- **Flamethrower:** The Iron Drake used a flamethrower against the party
- **Flight:** Could fly and carry passengers
- **Deformed Wings:** Described as having deformed wings that made flying awkward

## Notable Encounters

- **January 24, 2026:** Party encountered the Iron Drake during the train/boulder incident. The drake attacked while the party was escaping from bandits.

## Defeated

The Iron Drake was defeated during the escape from the train incident.
