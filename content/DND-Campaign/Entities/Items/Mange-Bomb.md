# Mange Bomb

**Type:** Magical Weapon / Consumable  
**Status:** Used

## Description

A dense compact magic item/weapon that releases magical energy when it detonates. The explosion enhances people's magical abilities for better or worse.

## Properties

- **Type:** Dense compact magic
- **Effect:** Releases magical energy into the air when detonated
- **Side Effect:** Enhances people's magical abilities (both positive and negative)

## Notable Events

- **Magic Enhancement:** After a mange bomb went off, people in the city started manifesting magical abilities - shooting sparks of flames and simple spells
- **Elysium's Analysis:** The mange bombs are "densely compact magic" that releases something into the air, enhancing magic

## Gameplay Significance

- Connected to the "Magic Awakening" in the city
- Preceded the random magic abilities appearing among citizens
- Magical arms race/enhancement event

## Notes

- Rare/unique magical item
- Used before current session timeline
