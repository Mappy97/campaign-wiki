# Whistlebrush Brigands

**Type:** Rival Outlaw Faction  
**Status:** Active

## Description

The Whistlebrush Brigands are a rival outlaw/mercenary group led by N. Gilder (General Gilder). They operate on the roads between Port Patara and the interior, and claim to work for the Mayor. Their territory overlaps with the party's operation area.

## Key Members

- **N. Gilder** (General Gilder) - Leader

## Notable Events

- **Bandit Hunt:** Patrols of Whistlebrush brigands have been seen looking for the party's bandits, forcing them to lay low.
- **Road Encounter:** Stopped the party on the road outside Port Patara, claiming mayoral authorization to question travelers. Dropped the act when the party used thieves' cant.
- **Ungors:** Deployed ungors (beastmen with hooves and arrows/spears) as fighters.

## Relationship with Party

- Complicated — they know each other from past dealings
- Use thieves' cant suggesting criminal underworld ties
- Both factions apparently work under the mayor

## Notes

- Use ungors as soldiers
- Claim mayor's sponsorship
- Patrol roads between Port Patara and the wilderness
