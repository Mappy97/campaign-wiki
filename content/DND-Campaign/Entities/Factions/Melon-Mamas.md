# Melon Mamas

**Type:** Rival Organization  
**Status:** Active

## Description

The Melon Mamas are a competing faction/organization that the party is in competition with. The party has considered impersonating tax collectors to interfere with the Melon Mamas' gold collection. They also had a bad encounter with a dragon (as part of the Witcher Quest).

## Notable Events

- **Competition:** Party is in direct competition with the Melon Mamas — sabotaging their "gold collection for the competition"
- **Tax Fraud Scheme:** Elysium suggested impersonating tax collectors to disrupt their operations: "we could impersonate tax collector with the Melon Mamas and potentially screw the Melon Mamas in their gold collection for the competition"
- **Dragon Encounter:** The Melon Mamas "had a bad encounter with the dragon or something" during the Witcher Quest
- **Field Stretchers:** During a major battle, Melon Mamas were seen dragging field stretchers made from scavenged materials — suggesting they were helping wounded

## Notes

- Direct competitors with the party
- Despite competition, may have allied temporarily during battles
- Connected to the Witcher Quest arc
