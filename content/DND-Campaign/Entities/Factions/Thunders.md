# The Thunders

**Type:** Enemy Faction

## Description

The Thunders are an enemy faction that the party has encountered multiple times. They are hostile and were involved in the train/boulder incident.

## Notable Encounters

- **Train/Boulder Incident (Jan 24, 2026):** The Thunders were trying to open fire on Gordon while he was behind a boulder. They were eventually knocked down by gusts of wind as the train crashed into the woods.

## Notes

- Enemies of the party
- Mentioned as being in pursuit of the party
