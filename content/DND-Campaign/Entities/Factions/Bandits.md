# Bandits

**Type:** Enemy Faction

## Description

A group of bandits encountered during the train/boulder incident. They were hiding near a boulder and captured Gary (the brute frog).

## Notable Events

- **Train/Boulder Incident (Jan 24, 2026):** Bandits were hiding near a boulder
- They captured Gary (Gordon's brute frog)
- They were sliding down the boulder with Gary when the party engaged
- Eventually defeated during the escape

## Notes

- Hostile to the party
- Associated with the location where Gary was captured
