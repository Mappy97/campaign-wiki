# The Deluge

**Type:** Secret Organization / Enemy Faction  
**Status:** Active

## Description

The Deluge is a secretive organization with spies embedded in the government and prison system. The party has encountered their operatives and worked against them.

## Known Activities

- **Prison Infiltration:** The Deluge had a spy working inside the prison walls ("somebody who was working the very walls of these government buildings within the prison itself")
- **Attack on Prison:** The Deluge was involved in an attack on the prison
- **Information Network:** Has spies in various government institutions

## Notable Events

- **Prison Mission:** The party conducted a mission at/in the prison, which the Deluge was involved with. The party received an envelope in appreciation for completing this mission.
- **Spy Capture:** A Deluge spy was captured and was being interrogated by the Royal Interrogator. The spy had been "subverting an interrogation."
- **Witch Hunter Warning:** At least one character warned about calling on the witch hunter to verify non-Deluge membership

## Relationships

- **The Party** — Opponents; party has worked against them
- **Royal Interrogator** — Interrogating their spy
- **Witch Hunter** — Enemy of the Deluge, used to verify loyalty

## Notes

- High-level organization with government penetration
- Their name suggests a flood/overwhelming force theme
- Connected to the prison attack backstory
