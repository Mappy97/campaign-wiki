# The Scaven

**Type:** Creature Faction / Underground Race  
**Status:** Active

## Description

The Scaven are rat-like creatures that live underground, digging tunnels and expanding their territory. They are in a perpetual underground war with other creatures (vortex). The scaven can overwhelm small towns and turn them to ruins.

## Nature

- Live and dig underground tunnels
- At war with "vortex" creatures underground
- Expand greedily, getting "more profound" and deeper
- Can overwhelm and destroy small towns

## Notable Events

- **Underground War:** "There has been a war waging underneath the ground for many, many years" between scaven and vortex
- **Tunnel Invasion:** Scaven invade vortex tunnels when they overexpand
- **Town Destruction:** Have destroyed towns, leaving them as ruins

## Connections

- The party appears to have some relationship with the scaven — someone called for "strong, valiant" leadership to "lead our scaven into the heat of battle"

## Notes

- Underground creatures
- Territorial and expansionist
- Connected to a deeper conflict the party is aware of
