# Session - June 3, 2025

**Date:** June 3, 2025 (multiple recordings: 1:12, 1:13, 1:53, 3:20 AM GMT = evening CST)  
**Participants:** Elysium, DM, Jimothy, Gordon

## Summary

The party traveled from the city (Port Patara gate) southeast toward the bandit camp, then into the forest to retrieve their podling companion, Frog Spittle, who had been held. They encountered Whistlebrush brigands on the road, used thieves' cant to defuse the situation, then reached the Podling Village where the deity Gleed manifested and healed Jimothy's scars.

## Key Events

### City Departure
- Party left through the **Patara Gate Bridge** heading southeast
- Noticed people in the city manifesting magic (after the Mange Bomb event) — guards were annoyed having been called in on their day off
- **Bandit Status:** 21 alive, 16 with upgraded weapons/armor, 10 injured (needing 3 days recovery)

### Road Encounter — Whistlebrush Brigands
- Encountered Whistlebrush brigands escorting shaken citizens who'd been attacked by **ungors** (beastmen with hooves, arrows, and spears)
- Brigands claimed to be "sponsored by the mayor himself" and asking questions
- Gordon and Elysium used **thieves' cant** to identify themselves — brigands recognized them and dropped their act
- **N. Gilder** and the brigands asked about Gordon's "shiny, fancy ring"

### Frog Spittle Rescue
- **Erasmus** had previously led bandits into the forest to retrieve Frog Spittle but they were shot with carefully aimed arrows (only to maim) and retreated
- Party traveled with Erasmus and Reyes (Arrayes) to retrieve Frog Spittle
- Frog Spittle led the party to the Podling Village through a hidden tunnel in tree roots

### Podling Village
- Met the **village guardian** who vouched Frog Spittle in ("you know the rules of the land")
- Gordon asked for help transforming back into a dwarf — Frog Spittle knew of a way
- **Deity Gleed** manifested: light cascaded down touching the podlings and the party; Jimothy's scars were healed
- The party were welcomed as guests
